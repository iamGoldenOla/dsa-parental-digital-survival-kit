Starting Digital Safety Conversations with Your Family

Parenting Tips
By Mr. Emeka Uzo
February 16, 2025
6 min read

Open conversations about digital safety are the foundation of a secure online experience for your family. Here's how to start and sustain these important discussions.

Share on Facebook Share on Twitter Share on LinkedIn Share on WhatsApp Share via Email
6 min read

Why Talk About Digital Safety?

Children are exposed to technology from an early age. Regular conversations help them understand risks, build trust, and empower them to make safe choices online.

How to Start the Conversation

  • Choose a relaxed, non-judgmental setting
  • Ask open-ended questions about their online experiences
  • Share your own digital habits and concerns
  • Listen actively and validate their feelings

Conversation Starters

  • What's your favorite thing to do online?
  • Has anyone ever made you feel uncomfortable online?
  • What would you do if you saw something upsetting on the internet?
  • How do you decide what's safe to share?
