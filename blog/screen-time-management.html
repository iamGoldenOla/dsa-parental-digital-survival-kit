Screen Time Management: Finding the Right Balance

Digital Wellness
By Dr. Funmilayo Adeyemi
February 10, 2025
6 min read

Managing screen time is a challenge for every modern family. Here are practical tips to help you set healthy boundaries and encourage balanced digital habits.

Share on Facebook Share on WhatsApp Share via Email
6 min read

Why Screen Time Matters

Excessive screen time can affect sleep, mood, and academic performance. Setting limits helps children develop healthy relationships with technology.

Tips for Parents

  • Set clear daily limits for each device
  • Use parental controls and monitoring tools
  • Encourage tech-free family activities
  • Model healthy screen habits yourself

Creating Tech-Free Zones

  • Bedrooms
  • Dining areas
  • During family time
